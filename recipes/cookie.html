Lasagna Recipes!

What do you need? You need:

  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Sauce
  • Butter
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocoate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)
    •

    Now, the steps!

    1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy.
    3. Beat in eggs and stir in vanilla.
    4. Disolve baking soda in hot water and add along with salt and mix until combined
    5. Stir in choco chips and nuts until soft dough forms.
    6. Drop rounded spoonfuls of cookie dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets and bake for ten minutes, until slightly brown.
    7. Cool on wire rack, transfer to airtight containers, and enjoy!
